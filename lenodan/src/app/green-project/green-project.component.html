LENODAN GREEN PROJECT

We are making changes throughout our company to actively address the challenges facing our industry and our planet.

Material

We source our fabrics and textile from local mills and suppliers. We work with local mills to produce our fabrics, which help to reduce the environmental impact associated with transportation. Additionally, we also source surplus fabrics from local suppliers, which helps to reduce textile waste.

Suppliers & Manufactures

We also work closely with a handful of small factories and suppliers that believe in transparency and are committed to fair working conditions. We believe that garment workers are the heartbeat of our production line, from concept to execution, and we are committed to ensuring that they are treated with dignity and respect. We also aim to associate ourselves with factories that are keen on working sustainably and that will help us to focus more on slow fashion to minimize waste. We believe that by taking a slow fashion approach, we can create fashion that has less social and environmental impact. We also take into consideration the impact of our decisions at the top management level to their effect on the worker at factory level.

Environmental & Social Issues

LENODAN is dedicated to taking environmental and social issues into consideration and has set it as our main responsibility to create high-end quality items that are true to our contemporary aesthetic, and are produced responsibly at all levels of the production cycle. We understand that the fashion industry has a significant impact on the environment and we are committed to doing our part to minimize that impact.

Packaging

We are committed to reducing our use of plastic. We understand that plastic waste is a major contributor to pollution and we are committed to minimizing our impact on the environment. We have eliminated the use of single-use plastics such as plastic bags and plastic wrapping in our packaging. Instead, we use paper, jute, and cotton to make our packaging materials, which are renewable, biodegradable, and compostable. Our commitment

We are aimed to become a socially responsible brand that is environmentally conscious and contributes positively towards building an industry with fair labor practices, empowered workers, and responsible packaging. We understand that sustainability is a journey and we are committed to continuously improving our practices and finding new ways to minimize our impact on the environment.

We believe that by making sustainable choices in every aspect of our business, we can create a more sustainable and fair fashion industry. From the materials we use to the factories we work with, we are committed to making conscious choices that align with our values and mission. We believe that by leading by example, we can inspire others in the fashion industry to do the same and help to create a more sustainable future for all.
